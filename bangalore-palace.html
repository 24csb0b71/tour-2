Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace, inspired by England’s Windsor Castle, is one of the most remarkable heritage landmarks in Bangalore. Built in 1878 by Chamaraja Wodeyar, the palace exhibits a unique blend of Tudor and Scottish Gothic architecture.

The interiors of the palace boast exquisite wooden carvings, breathtaking paintings, and elegant chandeliers that reflect the grandeur of the bygone era. The palace is surrounded by lush green gardens and is a popular venue for cultural events and concerts.

Visitors can take a guided tour to explore the palace’s history, architecture, and artifacts that give insights into the royal lifestyle of the Wodeyar dynasty.
